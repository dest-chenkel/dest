
# DEST Axioms & Ontology — v0.1

> **Theory:** DEST — Discrete Electromagnetic Spacetime Theory  
> **Principle:** One compact axiom set; all forces emerge from EM geometry, local updates, and Θ‑curvature.

## A. Primitive State & Units
- **Cell state (per tick):** `(E_mag, B_mag, S_ticks, T_ticks, direction)` with integers for EB/action and `direction ∈ S²`.
- **Action quantum:** each **actualize** spends `ΔS = h`; **drift** rotates `direction`, transporting near‑null at **c**.
- **Quanta:** electric step **ε₀=(1/3)e**; flux quantum **Φ₀=h/(2e)**; invariant **c**.

## B. Update Law (Drift/Actualize)
At each tick and cell: (1) **Drift** — rounded EB increments so accumulated ratios match free propagation; (2) **Actualize** — when finite‑change exceeds elasticity `ε(x)`, apply local reconfiguration and spend `ΔS=h`.

## C. Θ‑Curvature (Effective Geometry)
**Θ(x)** = expected actualize ticks per affine length; sustained Θ patterns act as **effective curvature** `g^eff` (redshift, bending, Shapiro‑like delay).

## D. Topology & Charge
**Charge** = global winding orientation of a **3‑anchor** toroidal EM loop; minimal stability requires 3 anchors.

## E. Species Primitives
Electron (single 3‑anchor loop); positron (mirror); photons (integer `n≥1`, **n=3** first robust; `n=1,2` unstable/metastable).

## F. Composition & Selection Rules
Pair creation/annihilation: `n₁+n₂=6` with thresholds and boundary clause; boundaries/composites can enable vacuum‑forbidden channels.

## G. Forces (previews)
EM native; **Θ→g^eff**; **strong** = multi‑loop confinement + phase‑lock; **weak** = loop unlocking + topology change with **n=1** drift packet.
