# YouTube Video Reference — DEST Historical Note (v0.1)

**Title:** “Is the Electron a Photon?”  
**Platform:** YouTube  
**URL:** https://youtu.be/f8O3XMrC8hg?si=DDXUnXuJq5jOdJpy  
**Viewed by author:** 15 April 2026
**Relevance to DEST:**  
This video prompted the investigation that led to the Williamson & van der Mark (1997)
paper on a toroidal‑topology photon model. That paper, in turn, inspired the earliest
conceptual exploration that became DEST.
